Polled at ~20 fps. All values are raw — the analyser does no EMA and no peak hold. Temporal smoothing for LED output happens per-binding in the modulator envelopes (effects/modulator.py), so each control picks its own attack/release. The three band rows are FFT-summed magnitudes over the configured fft_window for low (20–250 Hz), mid (250 Hz–2 kHz), and high (2–12 kHz) ranges.
